About Us

Culturing of Catfish is an integral part of Aquaculture and production of food. Raising our catfish under the best conditions has given us quality product. We also add value to our products by processing of our fish as this meets the demands of a unique audience

We're Uzama Enterprise and we've been processing and raising quality catfish to marketable sizes in Lagos since 2018. Together we established a company dedicated to sourcing for quality fingerlings and juveniles culturing them to market size and providing great services to our customers at a fair price.

We specialize in smoking and raising quality catfish giving customers value for their money. We love quality and ensure our products stands out in the market. Our keen interest in aquaculture drove us into setting up our own catfish management. We look forward to being a player of choice in catfish farming in Nigeria and Africa at large.
